about.

I’m a graphic and web designer based in Toronto, Canada. In 2016, I took my passion for design to the next level and began freelancing for clients across various industries. While studying at Humber College, I have taken my skills and expertise in graphic, motion and web design to the next level, with specific focus on branding and visual identity.

My personal style is clean, minimalistic yet impactful, transforming complex problems into effective design solutions. Whenever I’m not busy tinkering around my Adobe tools, you can find me reading or on the road with my family exploring nature.
